How it works

Advanced electrolysis technology in a clear three-step flow.

Step 1

Water enters the electrode chamber

Hard water passes through a stainless-steel chamber fitted with titanium-based electrodes.

Step 2

Calcium and magnesium are separated

The electrolysis process helps capture the minerals responsible for scale, corrosion, and buildup.

Step 3

Softened water moves through your system

You get water that is gentler on plumbing, fixtures, and daily use without salt, resin, or chemical additives.
